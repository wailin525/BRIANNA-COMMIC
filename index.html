About Author

Brian

Co-creator & storyteller

👆 tap to learn more

Brian is an 11-year-old boy and the storyteller behind the BriAnna Comic. He loves creating fun and imaginative stories full of adventure, humor, and creativity. Brian enjoys reading, brainstorming new comic ideas, and working together with Anna to bring their characters to life. He hopes readers will laugh and have fun while reading their comics. 📖✨

Anna

Co-creator & illustrator

👆 tap to learn more

Anna is a 9-year-old girl and the illustrator of BriAnna Comic! She loves drawing colorful characters, making funny jokes, and adding creative details to every comic. Anna enjoys expressing her imagination through art and always comes up with silly and cheerful ideas. She is proud to be part of BriAnna Comic and hopes everyone enjoys the stories and artwork as much as she does. 🎨🌸

About BriAnna Comics

BriAnna Comics is the home for stories created by kids. Every comic here was originally hand-drawn and hand-written on paper — then carefully turned into an e-book so friends and family can read along.

EMILY, mixes high school worries with secret swords and alien invasions.
PAUL vs. MOM ROBOT 3000, sends a purple superhero cat to fight a robot mom — with a giant Brianna in the final round!
THE ELEMENTAL KID, takes four magical kids through a stormy ocean to face mysterious ant-people and a spiky-tooth bread-person.
A POTION NAMED PO-PO, follows a boy whose magic potion turns him into something... unexpected. 💩
A POTION NAMED PO-PO · PART 2, continues the epic battle — with guns, growth, and even more potions! 🧪
KIRBY IN THE DARK WORLD, sends a pink fluffy hero into a dark hole, an underwater home, and a wild Flip-O-Rama showdown! ⭐
CINNAMOROLL'S STORY, follows a brave little pup who flies up to a thundercloud to rescue his Mom and Dad from an evil guy! 🌸
All seven are funny, brave, and full of imagination — the way only kids can write.
