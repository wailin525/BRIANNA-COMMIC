About BriAnna Library

BriAnna Library is the home for stories created by Brian (Zhaoyang) and Anna (Zhaoyi). Every comic here was originally hand-drawn and hand-written on paper — then carefully turned into an e-book so friends and family can read along.

Their first published comic, EMILY, mixes high school worries with secret swords and alien invasions. It's funny, brave, and full of imagination — the way only kids can write.
